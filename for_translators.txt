Translation notes for translators!

Thank you for your interest in translating SpireQuests! Here are some useful notes that we think will be useful for your translation.
Content here are divided by quest maker, then by quests.
Search for the quest maker's folder name in this document, then find the quest ID in their section.
Alternately, you can CTRL + F and find the ID of the item you are translating to see if someone has made a note on it.

VampireInTrainingQuest:
Quest and reward make reference to the vanilla "Vampires(?)" event. If you can make this more interesting in another language, please do.
Â  in English relic description is necessary to avoid the "any card with 'Strike' in its name" tag. The relic transforms only starter Strikes.

PrioritiesQuest:
Sarcastic remark suggesting that something is being done in exactly the _wrong_ order due to malice or incompetence. Also takes the form of "but... priorities." Rename as you see fit.
Regarding "One at a time" in quest description: at this time, defeating two or more enemies in a single action will fail the quest. This might be changed in a future revision.

RapidImprovisationQuest:
Player is compelled to play 6-10 random cards in one combat, often at a disadvantage. Quest could have been named "MacGyver Quest" for similar effect.
You could also change the theme entirely if there's a clever insect metaphor available.
The name and description of "Ecdysis" should be on theme with "Metamorphosis" and "Chrysalis". Note that "Ecdysis" might sound foreign even to a native English speaker (it's not even in IntelliJ's dictionary).

[ramchops]
BloomerQuest:
The quest's name is a play on the "Okay, Boomer" phrase and the "Mark of the Bloom".

CharityQuest:
"Charity Case" refers to someone pitiful you feel sorry for, but it's also a play on the word "Charity" itself.
The quest's text refers to The Cleric.
Make sure to check that the name is consistent with the base game.

EvilSentryQuest:
(FriendSentry)
The reward relic for this quest has flavor text that refers to The Architect, whose name should appear flavor text of base game relics relics such as the Coffee Dripper or the Calpiers.
Make sure to check that the names stay consistent.

HashtagBlessedQuest:
(BlessingEarth, BlessingHeaven, BlessingMan)
The cards from this quest's relic reward are references to Mahjong hands.
Please see the following for more details:
http://mahjong.wikidot.com/blessing-of-heaven
http://mahjong.wikidot.com/blessing-of-earth
http://mahjong.wikidot.com/blessing-of-man

SelloutQuest:
(SelloutAdCard)
Many strings in the Advertisement Card refers to characters or locations that already have established, translated names.
Be sure to check that the names stay consistent.
This card also refers to The Packmaster, whose translated names can be seen in https://github.com/erasels/PackmasterCharacter/tree/main/src/main/resources/anniv5Resources/localization.
Feel free to make up a translation if The Packmaster hasn't been translated in your language yet.
Jelly G. and UPDATE BODY TEXT are in-jokes from the SlayTheSpire modding community.

ThatsSmoresQuest:
(SmoresRelic)
The quest's name is a reference to the song That's Amore.
The quest reward's flavor text is a reference to the movie The Menu.
